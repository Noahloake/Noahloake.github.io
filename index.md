<h2 style="margin: 60px 0px 10px;">About</h2>

Welcome to my website! I am <b><i>Noah Loake</i></b>, a second-year Ph.D. student in Economics at the <i><b>Department of Applied Economics, Guanghua School of Management, Peking University</b></i> and I expect to graduate in June 2027. 

My research interests lie in leveraging large-scale datasets and combining design-based and model-based approaches to explore topics in <i><b>Political Economics, Development Economics, Public Finance</b></i> and <i><b>Macro Finance</b></i>.
